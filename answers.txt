Q1: Why might we consider using Git?


Answer: Using git helps one keep track of all changes made to a project. 
It is especially useful when working with teams.



Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: One particular field data science could be applied to is finance. 
Specifically, data science could be used in finance to gather information 
about stocks and train trading algorithms on it.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer:Merging combines two branches by creating a new merge commit, preserving
the full commit history. Rebasing moves commits from one branch onto another,
rewriting history to create a cleaner structure.
